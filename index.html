About Us

Empowering Cricket Fans through Decentralized Gaming

SIXR is the first Web3-powered ecosystem for cricket fans. Built on the ultra-fast TON blockchain with multi-chain distribution, SIXR delivers real-time competitive cricket action with full digital ownership, a play-to-earn and play-to-own model, and seamless DeFi integration. Superstar players engage with the games users and offer an interactive and engaging experience. With NFTs, dual tokens, and a creator economy, SIXR empowers players to earn, trade, and participate in governanceright from within the app they already use.
